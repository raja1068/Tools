Optimize Your PDFs for Better Performance

Our free online PDF compression tool helps you reduce the file size of your PDF documents while maintaining quality. Smaller PDFs are easier to share via email, upload to websites, and store in the cloud.

Why Compress PDFs?

  • Email attachments: Many email services have size limits for attachments
  • Faster uploads/downloads: Smaller files transfer more quickly
  • Website optimization: Reduce page load times when linking to PDFs
  • Storage savings: Store more documents in limited space
  • Mobile friendly: Smaller files use less data on mobile devices

How to Use Our PDF Compressor

  1. Upload your PDF by dragging and dropping or clicking to browse
  2. Adjust compression settings based on your needs
  3. Click "Compress PDF" to process your file
  4. Download the optimized PDF

Compression Techniques

Our tool uses advanced techniques to reduce PDF file size:

  • Image optimization: Compresses embedded images while preserving quality
  • Font subsetting: Includes only the used characters from fonts
  • Object compression: Efficiently compresses PDF objects and streams
  • Metadata removal: Optionally removes unnecessary metadata
  • Color space conversion: Converts colors to more efficient spaces when possible
